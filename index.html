Welcome

Welcome to my academic profile. I am Dr. Bidyapati Kumar, serving as a State Aided College Teacher (SACT) in the Department of Chemistry at Achhruram Memorial College, Jhalda, Purulia. I completed my M.Sc. in Organic Chemistry from Ranchi University in 2008 and M.Phil. in Chemistry from Ranchi University in 2019. I completed my Ph.D. in Chemistry from Sidho-Kanho-Birsha University, Purulia in 2026.

My teaching and research interests include Organic Chemistry and Chemical Education. I am passionate about teaching, mentoring students, and contributing to the advancement of chemical sciences through research.

🔭 About Research

My research focuses on the design and synthesis of metal complexes and their supramolecular activities, with special emphasis on catalytic applications (homogeneous and heterogeneous), HER, OER, enzyme mimic activity and enzyme sensing materials for environmental monitoring and biological use. Additionally, I am interested in biodiesel and other sustainable chemical processes.
